My Core Values

I believe it's important to start each task or day with these values in mind. Without them, life would be nowhere near as exciting as it could be, and work would be a drag!    

Creativity

I like to think that I am constantly excercising creativity - whether it be in code, writing, or media; 'unsolvable' problems become a lot more managable when you take a step back and look at all possible approaches. 

Growth Mindset 

Every task, whether it is successful or not, is an opportunity to learn something new and to grow as an individual. By accepting the growth mindset, we are able to stay determined through hardship and come back stronger than ever.      

Hard Work

No matter what you are working on, give it your all! I try to make sure each project I work on is my personal best; and by doing so, I am always proud of whatever I am able to accomplish.                 

Positive Attitude

Most importantly, its important to stay positive and have fun with whatever you are doing. Positivity brings a sense of fulfulment to whatever you do - if you enjoy your job, is it really work?
