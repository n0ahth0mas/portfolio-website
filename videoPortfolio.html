Hard Light

Hard Light was my senior thesis project for both the University of Puget Sound's Honors Program as well as my Digital Media & Communication Major. This transmedia webseries was to take place over YouTube and Twitter, but the school shutdown due to COVID-19 left me missing 60-70% of the footage. Because of this, I had to improvise and combined my prerecorded footage with stock elements, images, voiceovers, and additionally shot materials to piece together the rest of the story.

The Future of Influencers

This video project was created for my Online Communication class' student blog. My goal was to create a Vox-style video informing my peers about an up-and-coming trend in social media known as virtual inflencers. This video also served as the start to my research prospectus for the class, which can be viewed on this website.
